Biography

Matías Ignacio Marambio Jiménez is currently a Special Researcher at SOKENDAI, the Graduate University for Advanced Studies, in Japan. He is studying for his PhD at the astronomical science program in the National Astronomical Observatory of Japan. Matías earned a bachelor’s degree in astronomy and a master’s in engineering sciences at UC Chile, specializing in astronomical instrumentation with emphasis on spectrographs and adaptive optics. Driven by an entrepreneurial spirit, he is also leading a startup project in laser communications. Building on his deep learning expertise acquired during his master’s studies, his PhD research focuses on applying artificial intelligence to enhance adaptive optics systems. His long-term goal is to serve as a bridge between Japan and Chile, fostering collaboration and advancing the global astronomical community.
